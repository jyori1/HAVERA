Beginner Exercises

We have several different exercises to choose from!

Daily Exercise

The Daily Exercises are customized for every single day of the week, Wednesday will routinely be your rest day. The exercises progressively get longer and harder as you transition from Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced. It is best recommended to start with the Beginner exercises within your first month. Then, transition to other levels when you feel the time is right!

Goal-Orientated Exercise

The Goal Orientated Exercises focus on specific parts of your body. You’ll be able to pick from either developing your legs, arms, and/or abs. From your choice, we’ll be able to gather exercises that are distinctly targeting those points. For each exercise in the routine, you’ll be given a 10 seconds rest between. Meanwhile, you complete as many reps as possible within your 30 seconds interval.

7-Minute Workout

In 12 exercises deploying only body weight, a chair and a wall, the 7-Minute Workout essentially combines a long run and a visit to the weight room into about seven minutes of steady discomfort — all of it based on science. You are supposed to do as many reps as possible for each exercise within each 30 second slot, this allows you to maximize the intensity of your exercise while also minimizing its duration. For more notable results, you can repeat this routine 2-3 times as a whole workout, yet such a high-intensity routine should only be done once every two days.
